🎮 GAME 4 — Attention Highlighter
Story: Exam Questions

“You know when you read an exam question…

And there’s one sentence like:
‘Except for which of the following…’

And suddenly everything else becomes irrelevant?”

Punchline
“Your brain just applied attention.

Highlighting what matters.
Ignoring the rest.”

🎤 SPEAKER NOTES — 05_Game4_Attention_Highlighter
🎤 Hook

“You don’t read every word on a page.
AI used to.”

🧠 Concept Explanation

“Early AI systems processed language one word at a time.

They read from left to right, slowly.
By the time they reached the end of a sentence, they had already forgotten the beginning.

This made understanding long sentences extremely hard.”

🧠 Core Idea: Attention

“Modern AI works differently.

Instead of reading word by word, it looks at the entire sentence at once.

Then it decides which words matter more than others.

This decision process is called attention.”

🎢 Fun Analogy

“Imagine standing in a crowded cocktail party.

Dozens of conversations are happening around you.

You don’t hear everything.
You filter out the noise.

When someone says your name, your attention locks on instantly.

That filtering mechanism is attention.”

🧠 What Attention Actually Does

“Every word checks every other word.

It asks:

‘Are you important to me?’

‘Should I care about you right now?’

‘Do you change my meaning?’”

🤓 Fun Fact

“The research paper that introduced this idea was titled ‘Attention Is All You Need.’

That single idea completely replaced older language models and enabled systems like ChatGPT.”

📜 Script / Key Talking Points

“Look at the text on screen.”

“Notice how it’s blurred.”

“As I move the cursor, certain words become clear.”

“Those highlighted words are where the model is focusing.”

“Different words get different importance.”

“This is how AI understands relationships, not just sequences.”

“Without attention, AI can memorize text.”

“With attention, AI can understand it.”
