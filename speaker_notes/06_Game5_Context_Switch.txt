🎮 GAME 5 — Context Switch
Story: ‘Nice’ Text Message

“If your boss texts you:
‘Nice.’

You panic.

If your friend texts you:
‘Nice.’

You relax.

Same word.
Very different meaning.”

Punchline
“Context is everything.

AI doesn’t store meanings.
It adjusts them.”

🎤 SPEAKER NOTES — 06_Game5_Context_Switch
🎤 Hook

“One word can mean completely different things…
depending on context.”

🧠 Concept Explanation

“AI does not store fixed dictionary definitions.

Instead, it represents meaning as numbers.

Those numbers change based on surrounding words.

That changing meaning is called context.”

🧠 Tokens and Meaning

“A word is broken into a token.

Each token has a meaning represented as a position in space.

When context changes, that position moves.”

🎢 Fun Analogy

“If I say: ‘I’m going to the bank’…

If I’m holding a fishing rod, you imagine a river bank.
If I’m holding a credit card, you imagine a financial bank.

Same word.
Different meaning.

Context decides.”

🧠 What the Model Is Doing

“The model is constantly asking:

‘What is this word connected to?’

‘What came before it?’

‘What comes after it?’”

🤓 Fun Fact

“Modern language models track thousands of context dimensions at once.

They instantly know that ‘Jaguar’ refers to a car in a sentence about speed, and an animal in a sentence about the jungle.”

📜 Script / Key Talking Points

“Here is the word on screen.”

“Watch what happens when I switch the context.”

“Notice how the values change.”

“The model isn’t confused.”

“It’s adapting its understanding.”

“This is why modern AI can follow conversation.”

“Meaning is not fixed.”

“Meaning is relational.”
