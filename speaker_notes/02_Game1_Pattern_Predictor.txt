🎤 SPEAKER NOTES — 02_Game1_Pattern_Predictor
🎤 Hook

“Let’s start simple.
Can you predict the future?
Or at least… the next number?”

🧠 Concept Explanation

“This game shows the most basic form of intelligence.

A single input goes in.
A single answer comes out.

The system isn’t reasoning.
It’s detecting a pattern and extending it.”

🎢 Fun Analogy

“Imagine judging how spicy a curry is.

The number of chilies goes in.
Some chilies are mild. Some are dangerous.
And everyone has a different tolerance.

Same input.
Different result.

That’s how a neuron works.”

🤓 Fun Fact

“Your brain has about 86 billion neurons doing this exact calculation right now — just to understand this sentence.”

📜 Script / Key Talking Points

“Here’s the pattern: 2 goes to 4. 3 goes to 6.”

“What should 5 go to?”

“Most of you saw the pattern instantly.”

“That’s all this system is doing.”

“This green dot is a neuron.”

“It takes a number, multiplies it, adds a small adjustment, and outputs a guess.”

“If you guessed wrong, that’s fine.”

“That’s called training data.”
