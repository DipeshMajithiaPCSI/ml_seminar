This game teaches **Linear Regression** in its simplest form (y = mx + c). But we call it "Pattern Recognition".
- **Input (x)**: The number given.
- **Output (y)**: The answer.
- **Weight (m)**: How much the input matters.
- **Bias (c)**: An extra nudge.
